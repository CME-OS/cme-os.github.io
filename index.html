What is Campaigns Made Easy?

Campaigns Made Easy aka (CME) is an open source email campaign system just like phpList and MailChimp that allows you to create email lists and send out campaigns.

Free & Open Source

This means you have full control over your email lists and campaigns. This makes CME a really cheap way to send email campaigns because its free to use

Easy to Install

CME is really easy to install. Just download it onto your web server. Point your browser to it and follow the installation wizard

Active Community

CME has an active and friendly community who contribute to the development and reliability of CME and are always happy to support its users

Multiple SMTP Providers

CME allows you to send campaigns through different SMTP providers. This gives you more flexibility as you can for example, send transactional emails through Mandrill and Campaigns through AWS!

Powerful Analytics Report

Track how your campaigns perform by seeing the number of clicks, bounce rate and unsubscribes.

Perfect for Business Owners

If you are a business owner with multiple brands. CME allows you to manage campaigns for all brands in one interface.
